Description

Shrimp scampi is a seafood dish made of shrimp cooked in a butter, garlic, and white wine sauce. This recipe can be made in many ways.
Everyone has a different version, but scampi usually includes these three ingredients. You can serve shrimp scampi by itself as an appetizer or over pasta as a main dish.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • White
  • Lemone juice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prep ahead
  2. Cook the pasta first
  3. Dont't overcook the shrimp
