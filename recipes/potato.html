Cake

Description

My potatoes Romanoff is an ideal dish for big holiday gatherings
since you can make it the day before and bake it when needed.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Shallots
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Bake and chill potatoes
  2. Shred Potatoes
  3. Bake
  4. Serve it
  5. Store it
  6. Save it
